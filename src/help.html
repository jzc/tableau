tableau

TL;DR: This is an interactive app demonstrating tableau proofs for propositional logic. Roughly, a tableau is a tree of formulae where the formulae occuring lower in the tree are logical consequences of the formulae above. The goal of a tableau proof is to find a contradiction in every branch of the tree; a contradiction is the formula \bot or a pair of formulae of the form p and \not p. Left click formulae to simplify them; if there are multiple branches below a formula you want to simplify, you will have to left click one of the highlighted branches below to the formula to apply the simplification to that branch. If \bot appears in your tableau, right clicking it will close all the branches it appears in. Otherwise, if you right click a formula other than \bot, then the formulae that share a branch with that formula will be highlighted. If you right click one the higlighted formulae such that the pair of formulae you right clicked are of the form \phi and \not \phi, then the branches that contain the formulae you selected be closed.
